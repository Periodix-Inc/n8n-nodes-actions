# Changelog

All notable changes to this project are documented in this file.

The format is based on [Keep a Changelog](https://keepachangelog.com/en/1.1.0/),
and this project adheres to [Semantic Versioning](https://semver.org/spec/v2.0.0.html).

## 1.1.0 – 2026-06-03

### Added

- `Periodix LinkedIn Get Profile` node: Fetch a full LinkedIn profile — experience, education, skills, and summary — for enrichment and personalization
- `Periodix LinkedIn Get Company Profile` node: Fetch a LinkedIn company profile — industry, size, description, and locations — for account research and enrichment
- `Periodix LinkedIn Get User Posts` node: List recent posts by a LinkedIn person or company — for pre-outreach research and personalization
- `Periodix LinkedIn Get Post Comments` node: List the comments on a LinkedIn post, with author details — a source of engaged leads
- `Periodix LinkedIn Get Post Reactions` node: List the people who reacted to a LinkedIn post or comment — a source of warm leads
- `Periodix LinkedIn Get Connections` node: List the 1st-degree connections of a connected LinkedIn account, for mutuals and re-engagement

## 1.0.0 – 2026-05-12

### Added

- `Periodix LinkedIn Search` node — runs LinkedIn Classic and Sales Navigator searches (People, Companies, Posts, Jobs) by URL
